Pinout 

2B – black
2A – brown
1A – blue
1B – red
MS1 – GND (via 100k resistor)
MS2 – VCC
MS3 – unconnected (means low, has internal pull down resistor)
RESET and SLEEP are connected

The connections from the Arduino are: 

pin 6 – Debug LED
pin 8 – Servo signal
pin 9 – Driver 1, DIR
pin 10 – Driver 1, STEP
pin 11- Driver 2, DIR
pin 12 – Driver 2, STEP
